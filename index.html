What is this?

This is a get-together to write HTML documents as a part of World HTML Day. Feel the HTML Energy.

Do I need to know HTML to join?

No, everyone is welcome and we will help each other out.

What should I bring?

  • something to write HTML on — a piece of paper, a computer, a typewriter, a chalkboard...
  • a picnic blanket
  • a snack to share

When, where?

August 8, 2026, 5-8pm, Plage de Vidy-Théâtre, 1007 Lausanne, Switzerland

Who is running this?

Chae and Teo

I'm not in Lausanne. Is there an event in my city?

Check out html.energy/events.html. If your city isn't listed, feel free to organize your own!

Non, tout le monde est le bienvenu et on s'entraide.

  • de quoi écrire du HTML — une feuille de papier, un ordinateur, une machine à écrire, un tableau noir...
  • une couverture de pique-nique
  • un en-cas à partager

8 août 2026, 17h–19h, Plage de Vidy-Théâtre, 1007 Lausanne, Suisse
